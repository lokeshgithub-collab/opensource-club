    Event Overview:

    The Open Source Club of B.S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science & Technology is thrilled to announce an exciting Code Challenge: Pre-Placement Prep. This two-week coding contest is designed to help students sharpen their programming skills and gear up for the upcoming placement season. Whether you are a seasoned coder or just starting, this challenge is the perfect opportunity to test your abilities and improve through consistent practice. Additionally, participants stand a chance to win amazing cash prizes, making this not just a learning experience but also a rewarding one!

    Event Details:

    • Duration: The contest runs from 19th August 2024 to 30th August 2024.
    • Daily Challenges: Each day, at 7 PM, a unique contest link will be shared with participants. This link will contain three coding challenges categorized as Easy, Medium, and Hard. The diverse difficulty levels ensure that everyone, from beginners to advanced coders, can participate and challenge themselves. Programming Language Flexibility: Participants are free to use any programming language they are comfortable with, allowing for maximum creativity and problem-solving flexibility.
    • Attendance: To qualify for the prizes, participants must attend the contest every day throughout the entire duration.

    Registration:

    Scan the QR code in the image or click the provided link to register for the challenge. Make sure to register as soon as possible to secure your spot in this competitive and educational event!
