About Us

Welcome to our Open Source Club, a vibrant community of passionate developers and designers. We focus on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, machine learning, and cutting-edge design practices. Whether you're a beginner eager to learn or an expert ready to share your knowledge, our club offers a collaborative space for everyone. We host workshops, hackathons, and coding sessions, encouraging creativity and innovation. Our mission is to empower members to contribute to open-source projects, build real-world skills, and foster a culture of learning and growth. Join us, and let's code a better future together!

Projects

This project involves crafting a professional and visually appealing resume using HTML and CSS. You'll learn how to structure content, apply styling, and create a responsive layout. The final product will showcase your skills, experience, and education in a clean, modern design that's easy to customize and update.

This project focuses on creating a simple navigation system between three pages: a home page, a black-themed page, and a white-themed page. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, you'll build interactive buttons that seamlessly switch between these pages, demonstrating fundamental web development skills in styling and user interaction.

In this project, you'll design a visually appealing product card using HTML and CSS. The card will showcase product details such as an image, title, description, and price. You'll focus on layout, typography, and responsive design to ensure the card looks great on various devices and screen sizes.
