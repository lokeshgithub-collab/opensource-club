About the Club

The Open Source Club at Crescent University is a community-driven organization focused on fostering knowledge and skills in open-source technologies. Our mission is to create a collaborative environment where students can learn, grow, and contribute to real-world projects.

We organize workshops, hackathons, and coding sessions that help members stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the tech industry. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, our club has something to offer for everyone.

What We Offer

  • Weekly Coding Challenges
  • Project-Based Learning
  • Mentorship Programs
  • Collaborative Open Source Projects
  • Guest Lectures from Industry Experts
  • Networking Opportunities
