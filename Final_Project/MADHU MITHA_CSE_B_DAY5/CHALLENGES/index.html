WELCOME TO THE OPEN SOURCE CLUB

Embark on a captivating voyage through the digital realm with the Open Source Club at BSA Crescent Institute of Science and Technology. Our club is your passport to an open and inclusive community where passion meets proficiency. Join us as we unravel the secrets of open-source wonders, and forging connections that will ignite your tech-driven aspirations. Elevate your knowledge, and set sail towards a future brimming with possibilities. The adventure begins here, where technology and openness converge in a symphony of boundless opportunities.

JOIN OUR COMMUNITY

Unlock Your Potential with the Open Source Club

Why Join Us?

  • Collaborative Learning: Learn and grow together with peers who are just as eager to explore new technologies and contribute to meaningful projects.
  • Hands-on Experience: Participate in workshops, hackathons, and coding sessions where you can work on real-world open-source projects.
  • Networking Opportunities: Build lasting connections with fellow students, professionals, and open-source enthusiasts. These networks can open doors to internships, jobs, and collaborative projects.
  • Impactful Projects: Contribute to projects that matter. Whether you're interested in web development, AI, cybersecurity, or any other field, there’s a place for you here.
  • Personal Growth: Enhance your problem-solving skills, learn to work in a team, and develop leadership qualities that will set you apart in your future career.
    •

Open Source Club is more than just a club – it’s a community of innovators, creators, and learners who are shaping the future of technology. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, there’s a place for you here.

Join us today and start your journey towards becoming a skilled and impactful member of the tech world.

Our Activities

Workshop 1:Organized an enriching Machine Learning workshop

  • Introduced participants to the fundamentals of Machine Learning and its applications.
  • Covered essential topics like supervised and unsupervised learning.
  • Provided hands-on experience with real-world datasets and projects.
  • Offered mentorship and guidance for further exploration in the field.
  • Engaged participants in interactive sessions to enhance their understanding and skills.
  • Conducted exciting and interesting quiz contest for the better interaction

DAY-1:

Introduction to the basic concepts of the Machine Learning and how it is ruling the tech world.

DAY-2:

Conducted exciting QUIZ CONTEST for better interaction.

DAY-3:

Diving into the deeper concepts,exploring the datasets and at last certificates distribution.

Workshop 2:Organized 5 Days of HTML,CSS,and BOOTSTRAP bootcamp

  • Introduced participants to the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.
  • Covered essential HTML topics, including tags, attributes, and semantic elements.
  • Explored CSS basics, such as selectors, properties, and the Box Model.
  • Provided hands-on experience in building responsive layouts using the Bootstrap grid system.
  • Engaged participants in interactive sessions to create navigation bars, cards, and forms with Bootstrap.
  • Demonstrated how to customize Bootstrap components with custom CSS for unique designs.
  • PROVIDED DAILY CHALLENGES IN GITHUB

Introduction to html, css and bootstrap by covering html tags ,css properties etc..

Conducted Daily Challenges and those challenges are posted in the github. These challenges are given for the better understanding of the concepts.

Leaderboard was maintained to appreciate the efforts.
